Section 2
=========

You can write math equations, too! 

An example inline equation is, $\alpha^2 = \beta^2 + \theta^2$.  Two standalone
math equations: 

$$I = \sum_{i=1}^N x_i$$

$$I = \frac{r}{y}$$

See more examples [here](http://pandoc.org/demos.html).
