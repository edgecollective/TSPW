Edge Collective

Open Farm Electronics: Easy & Hackable Sensing, Logging and Control

Easy, robust, hackable. Edge Collective's tools are easy to get up-and-running, but are also easy to modify for your own custom projects.

Plays nice with others. Our prototypes work out-of-the-box, but can also be re-programmed via the Arduino IDE or via Micropython -- through a wired connection, or via a web-interface. They can send data to Adafruit.io, FarmOS.org, ThingSpeak, or your own custom server.

Freedom to DIY. We sell fully-assembled kits, but our designs are fully open source: we list out all of the ingredients, so you can buy the necessary parts and produce your own hardware. If you do, we'd love for you to 'share back' and collaborate with us -- but you don't have to! :)

Germination Monitoring Kits

A simple, wireless system that provides online measurement and control for your germination setup. Features include:

  • Easy connection to your wifi router
  • Long-range radio options in case of no wifi
  • Wide voltage input range (up to 36 Volts)
  • Ability to retrofit existing germination control systems
  • (And more ...)

Wireless Dataloggers

The Quahog is an ESP32-based, Micropython board designed for robust and resilient datalogging and actuation projects. Applications include sensing and control for:

  • Germination
  • Soil health
  • Greenhouse monitoring
  • Third-party sensors and control systems
  • (And more ...)
